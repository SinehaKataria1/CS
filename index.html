COVID-19 inquiry

The UK COVID-19 Inquiry has been set up to examine the UK's preparedness and response to the COVID-19 pandemic, and to learn lessons for the future.
Members want to look forward, ensuring that lessons learned are applied to policies and plans across health and social care. Members wishing to submit evidence to the inquiry can do so via our online form.
